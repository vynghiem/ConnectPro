About ConnectPro

At ConnectPro we understand that every moment counts and that making the most of it means you can become busy. With ConnectPro we offer you a way to be able to keep track of what is most important without losing any of your time. Designed to work on phones, laptops, and desktops, ConnectPro is ready to help no matter what type of machine your using. Understanding the importance of privacy we at ConnectPro have designed our application to be non-intrusive. Furthermore, any information that is asked for is used only for the sole purpose of the applications function.
